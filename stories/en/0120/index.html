Hair Haare Hair

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath

Darshan Soni

Englisch

Level 1

Thuli has short hair.

Thuli hat kurze Haare.

Thuli has short hair.

Anna has long hair.

Anna hat lange Haare.

Anna has long hair.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Cathy hat die längsten Haare.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Zama hat ihre Haare geflochten.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Baba has a beard.

Baba hat einen Bart.

Baba has a beard.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Zanele hat ihre Haare gekämmt.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Thabo hat seine Haare geschnitten.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Themba hat seine Haare rasiert.

Themba has shaved his hair.
