Storybooks Deutschland

Storybooks Deutschland is a free open educational resource that promotes literacy and language learning in homes, schools, and communities. It makes 40 stories from the African Storybook available with text and audio in German, English, and the most widely spoken languages of Germany. Share and enjoy!
