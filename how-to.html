Hilfe

  • Click on a story to read it.
  • Click on "change language" to read the story in a different language.
  • Click on the icon to hear the story read aloud. (Please note that not all stories have audio versions.)
  • Click on de and en icons to switch quickly between German and English, respectively. (Please note that this only changes the language of the text. Click on Change language to change the language of the audio as well.)
